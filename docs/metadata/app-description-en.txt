Cisum - A Clean, Ad-Free Audio Player

A pure music player for ad-free, focused listening. Supports local music and audiobooks.

Cisum is a player designed for those who truly love music. No annoying ads, no shake-to-navigate, no endless popups—just a pure music experience.

Key Features
• Local music playback - Supports MP3, M4A, FLAC, WAV and other mainstream formats
• Audiobook management - Independent book library, easily manage audio content
• iCloud sync - Music library stays synchronized across all devices
• Clean interface - Following Apple design guidelines for a native experience

Why Choose Cisum?

Most music apps seem to care more about ads and monetization than your listening experience. Cisum is different—we believe a good player should make you forget its existence and focus only on the music itself.

Perfect For
• Music lovers tired of ads
• Users with large local music collections
• Audiobook/podcast heavy users
• People seeking a simple, efficient experience
